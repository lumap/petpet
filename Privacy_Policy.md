Data is not sold, provided to, or shared with any third party, except where required by law or a Terms of Service agreement. You can view the data upon request from `lumap@duck.com`.

The Bot collects Discord User IDs and stores them in a temoorary array. The Bot does not collect any other data. However no guarantee is provided and the Bot owners assume no liability for the unintentional or malicious breach of Data. In the event of an unauthorised Data access, users will be notified through the Discord client application.

## User Rights

At any time, you have the right to request to view the Data pertaining to your Discord account. You may submit a request through [email](mailto:lumap@duck.com). You have the right to request the removal of relevant Data.


## Questions

If you have any questions or are concerned about what data might be being stored from your account contact `lumap@duck.com`. For more information check the [Discord Terms Of Service](https://discord.com/terms).
